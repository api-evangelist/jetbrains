JetBrains AI supports multiple coding agents, including Junie, Codex, Claude Agent, and any ACP-compatible agent you bring yourself. Previously, AI users in JetBrains IDEs started in Chat mode and had to choose an agent themselves. As models became more advanced, agents became more capable and their adoption grew.
